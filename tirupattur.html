Yelagiri hill


Yelagiri top can be reached by a winding ghat road, that has 14 hairpin bends. The seventh hairpin bend is significant, since it offers the view of the slop of the mountain and the green forest covering as a carpet of the hill. Jalagamparai Falls



At a distance of 13 km from Thirupattur, 37 km from Yelagiri, & 37 from Athanavur, Jalagamparai Waterfall is a magnificent waterfall situated at Jadayanur near Yelagiri Hills. It is one of the must include places in Yelagiri Hills holiday packages.


Nilavoor Lake

NilavoorNilavoor Lake is actually a temple pond. It is located at almost the end of the road in Nilavoor after which you have a couple of viewpoints. There are no commercial activities here, so it has remained clean.
